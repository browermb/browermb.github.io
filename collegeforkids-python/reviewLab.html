College for Kids - Python: Review Lab

In this lab you will be using turtle to review what we have learned.

It is your choice as to what you create. However, there are some requirements. Your program needs to have at least one function, it needs to have at least one loop, also you need to ask for user input. Bonus points if you can add an if statement in your program!

If you have already finished the lab wait patiently for your classmates to finish. We will take a break and then learn our new lesson before starting your final project.
